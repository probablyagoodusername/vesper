Good. You're grounded. And today, before you step into anything, you're going to prepare. Not with a checklist. With something older and stronger.

Paul wrote to the church in Ephesus... a small community under real pressure, navigating real conflict, both visible and invisible... and he told them this:

"Be strong in the Lord and in his mighty power. Put on the full armor of God, so that you can take your stand against the devil's schemes. For our struggle is not against flesh and blood, but against the rulers, against the authorities, against the powers of this dark world."

This isn't metaphor for the passive. This is preparation for someone entering the arena. And today... you suit up. Not in arrogance. In readiness. Piece by piece.

[10s pause]

Start with the belt of truth. Feel your core... your center of gravity. Tighten it slightly. Engage those muscles. Truth is what holds everything else in place. It's the anchor piece. Without honesty... with yourself, with God, with the people around you... nothing else fits right. Every other piece depends on this one.

Lord, make me honest today. With my words, with my motives, with myself. Where I'm tempted to pretend, give me the courage to be real.

[10s pause]

Now the breastplate of righteousness. Take a deep breath and feel your chest expand. This covers your heart... the most vulnerable thing you carry. And righteousness here isn't perfection. It's right relationship. With God. With others. With yourself. Your worth isn't in your performance today. It never was. It's in whose you are.

Lord, guard my heart. When I'm tempted to earn my worth through output, remind me that my identity is already secure in you.

[10s pause]

Your feet. Press them into the ground, wherever you are. Feel the stability. The weight of your body held by the floor beneath you. These are feet fitted with the readiness that comes from the gospel of peace. You are ready to go where you're needed today. Not to fight people... to bring peace where there's conflict. To carry calm into chaos.

Lord, make me a peacemaker. Give me steady feet and a willingness to go where I'm sent.

[10s pause]

Now the shield of faith. Hold out your left hand, palm facing forward. This is what stands between you and the flaming arrows... the sudden doubts, the whispered accusations, the fear that shows up without warning. Not a shield of certainty. A shield of trust. You don't need to see the whole path. You don't need to understand every detail. Just the next step. Faith is enough for that.

Lord, I trust you with what I cannot see and what I cannot control. Strengthen my grip on this shield today.

[10s pause]

The helmet of salvation. Touch your forehead gently with your fingertips. Feel the warmth of your own hand against your skin. Your mind is protected. The anxious spirals, the self-doubt, the comparison, the replaying of old failures... they hit the helmet, not your mind. Lord, guard my thinking. When the lies come... and they will... help me recognize them for what they are. And help me choose truth instead.

[10s pause]

Not against people... never against people... but against the lies that say you're not enough, that today will overwhelm you, that you are alone in this fight.

Lord, make your Word alive in me today. Bring the right verse to mind at the right moment. And give me the courage to speak it.
