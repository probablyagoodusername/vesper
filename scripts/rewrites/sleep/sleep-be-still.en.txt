[15s pause]

This verse comes from a psalm about chaos. The nations rage. The kingdoms totter. The earth melts. It is not a psalm about a peaceful afternoon. It is a psalm about a world falling apart, and in the middle of the noise and the destruction, a voice says... be still. Stop. The command is given not to the chaos but to the listener. You cannot stop the world from shaking. But you can stop your own frantic response to it. Be still. And in the stillness... know.
