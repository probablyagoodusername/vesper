"And the peace of God, which surpasses all understanding, will guard your hearts and your minds in Christ Jesus."

Guard. The Greek word is phroureo... a military term. It means to garrison. To post sentries around. The peace of God stands watch tonight so that you don't have to. Your mind can stop patrolling the perimeter. You are off duty.

The method is simple. I'll name parts of your body, one by one, and all you do is move your awareness there. You don't need to relax that part. You don't need to do anything to it. Simply notice it, the way you'd notice a room you're walking through. The noticing itself is the practice.

If you fall asleep during this... that's fine. That's the point. Wherever you stop, the work is done.

Now we practice paired opposites. The nervous system releases its deepest tension when it experiences one state, then its opposite, and then neither. This is how the body remembers what neutral feels like.
