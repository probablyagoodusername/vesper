The physiological sigh. It works by fully inflating your lung's alveoli on the double inhale, then offloading excess CO2 on the long exhale, which directly activates your parasympathetic nervous system.

Two quick inhales through the nose. One long exhale through the mouth. Six cycles. I'll guide you.
